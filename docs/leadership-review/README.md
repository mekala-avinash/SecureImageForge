# Leadership Review Package

Two-document review kit for platform leadership to **approve phasing and FTE allocation** for the Enterprise Platform Transformation program.

| Document | Read first | Purpose | Approx. read time |
|---|---|---|---|
| [`EXECUTIVE_BRIEFING.md`](./EXECUTIVE_BRIEFING.md) | ⭐ Yes | Decisions, dollars, risks, FTE — at-a-glance briefing | 15 min |
| [`PHASING_AND_FTE_APPROVAL.md`](./PHASING_AND_FTE_APPROVAL.md) | After briefing | Formal decision register + sign-off block | 10 min |

### Suggested review flow (1-hour leadership meeting)

| Time | Item | Who |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:10 | Walk-through of Executive Briefing §1–§4 (Ask, Why, Target, Phases) | VP Platform |
| 0:10–0:25 | FTE allocation, hiring delta, budget envelope (§5 + Approval §4–§5) | VP Platform + VP HR + CFO |
| 0:25–0:35 | Compliance scope & sequencing (Yr1→Yr3) | CISO + Head of Compliance |
| 0:35–0:45 | Risk posture + phase gates | VP Platform + Head of SRE |
| 0:45–0:55 | Decision register walkthrough (D-1 … D-12) | All approvers |
| 0:55–1:00 | Sign approvals or capture conditions | All approvers |
