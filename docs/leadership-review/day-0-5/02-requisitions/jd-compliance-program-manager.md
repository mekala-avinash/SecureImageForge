# Compliance Program Manager

> Req ID: GRC-2026-001 · Team: Governance, Risk & Compliance · Reports to: Head of Compliance
> Location: Remote · Level: M1 / Senior IC or first-line manager track

## About the role
Own the compliance program that runs alongside our 12-month platform transformation. You will sequence and deliver SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 (Year 1), PCI-DSS + HIPAA (Year 2), and FedRAMP Moderate readiness (Year 3). You will partner with platform engineering to ensure compliance is a *byproduct of doing the work*, not a separate scramble.

## What you'll work on
- **Audit calendar**: sequence audits across SOC 2 / ISO / PCI / HIPAA / FedRAMP; manage external auditors and internal evidence delivery.
- **Continuous evidence**: onboard and operate a compliance automation platform (Drata / Vanta / Secureframe — see [`05-compliance-platform-rfp/`](../05-compliance-platform-rfp/)). Replace manual evidence gathering with automated pulls from K8s, IdP, Vault, cloud, MDM.
- **Control mapping**: maintain the cross-framework control matrix; coordinate with DevSecOps to align technical controls to framework requirements.
- **Risk management**: own the enterprise risk register; quarterly risk reviews.
- **Vendor & third-party risk**: DPIA, SCC, vendor assessments for all integrations (Drata, Splunk, etc.).
- **Access reviews**: quarterly, automated from SCIM + RBAC.
- **DSAR (GDPR) workflow**: 30-day SLA, automated intake via Backstage.

## Must-have
- 5+ years GRC experience with at least one full lead-from-the-front SOC 2 Type II cycle.
- Hands-on with at least one compliance automation platform (Drata, Vanta, Secureframe, Sprinto).
- Working understanding of cloud-native architectures (Kubernetes, GitOps, IAM, KMS, mesh).
- Comfortable reading control language (SOC 2 TSC, ISO 27001:2022 Annex A, NIST 800-53).
- Strong project management; can drive 50+ controls and 10+ stakeholders without dropping balls.

## Nice-to-have
- PCI-DSS QSA experience or formal training.
- HIPAA covered-entity / business-associate experience.
- FedRAMP package contribution (3PAO collaboration).
- ISO 27001:2022 Lead Implementer.
- CISA / CISM / CRISC.

## What success looks like in 12 months
- SOC 2 Type II report issued, unqualified.
- ISO 27001 certification achieved.
- Drata (or equivalent) live, pulling continuous evidence from ≥ 12 source systems.
- Auditor portal in place; auditor on-site days reduced ≥ 70% via automation.
- PCI / HIPAA Year-2 scope plan delivered and signed off.

## What we offer
- A program that already has executive sponsorship and a written architecture (read `/docs/`).
- Direct partnership with engineering — controls are *designed in*, not bolted on.
- Budget for tooling, training, and external auditor engagement.
