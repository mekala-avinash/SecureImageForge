# Senior Platform Engineer (×3)

> Req ID: PLAT-2026-001/002/003 · Team: Platform Engineering · Reports to: VP Platform Engineering
> Location: Remote (with quarterly on-sites) · Level: IC4 / Senior · Hiring manager: <Platform Lead>

## About the role
You will be a core builder of ACME's next-generation enterprise platform. We're transforming the company from a single-region monolithic stack into a multi-region, multi-tenant, regulated-ready, GitOps-driven platform with autonomous engineering agents. This role sits at the center of that transformation.

## What you'll work on
- **GitOps & Kubernetes**: own ArgoCD ApplicationSets, Argo Rollouts canary patterns, cluster bootstrap (Crossplane, Cluster API), multi-region topology, namespace tenancy model.
- **Hardened runtimes**: distroless / Wolfi-based language images for Node, Python, Go, Rust, Java, Ruby, PHP, .NET; SBOM + Cosign + SLSA L3 attestations.
- **Internal Developer Platform**: build and extend Backstage — service catalog, scaffolders (golden paths), TechDocs, cost/SLO tabs.
- **Service mesh & networking**: Istio multi-primary mTLS, Cilium default-deny, SPIRE workload identity.
- **Observability**: OpenTelemetry SDK integration, Prometheus rules, Loki/Tempo pipelines, SLOs-as-code.
- **Developer experience**: paved roads that make the secure path the easy path.

## Must-have
- 7+ years software engineering, with 3+ years deep on Kubernetes (operators, RBAC, network policy, CRDs).
- Production experience with at least one of: Argo CD, Flux, Crossplane.
- Strong with **at least two** of: Go, Python, Rust, TypeScript.
- Experience writing and operating Helm charts and/or Kustomize overlays at scale.
- Working understanding of container security: distroless/Wolfi, Cosign, SBOMs, CVE management.
- Practiced with IaC: Terraform / OpenTofu (or Pulumi / Crossplane).
- Strong written communication — we are a docs-first team.

## Nice-to-have
- Backstage plugin development experience.
- Service mesh ops (Istio / Linkerd) at production scale.
- Multi-region / multi-cloud topologies.
- Experience with progressive delivery, feature flags, ephemeral environments.
- Familiarity with SLSA, in-toto, Sigstore.
- Contributions to CNCF projects or significant OSS work.

## What success looks like in 12 months
- You've led at least one of the major Phase 1/2 workstreams end-to-end (e.g., the hardened runtime program, the multi-region rollout, the Backstage golden paths).
- New services from Backstage scaffold reach production via signed builds + GitOps in < 1 hour.
- You're a multiplier — paved roads you build are adopted by ≥ 80% of new services.

## What we offer
- A 12-month roadmap with explicit scope, exec sponsorship, and budget.
- Greenfield platform work backed by an architecture blueprint.
- Best-of-class CNCF stack (no proprietary lock-in).
- Mentorship from staff/principal engineers.
